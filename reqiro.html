Leading People and projects

Reqiro - formerly Planning & Logistics, is a leading project management consultancy to clients in the power/energy, infrastructure/train and industrial sectors. As the business evolved and more offerings were developed, the identity and brand strategy needed to reflect the new scope of services while positioning them as an attractive partner for both client and consultant in a competitive market. Working closely with the client, we developed a new vision, brand platform, tone of voice and visual identity that represents Reqiro's values and focus on team spirit and skills development.
